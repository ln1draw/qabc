Pastoral Staff

Reverend Sean Brown was called to Queen Anne Baptist Church in March of 2014 as an interim pastor. Sean was born and raised in Seattle and considers himself a true Northwesterner. Sean's undergraduate work was in ancient history at the University of Washington, and he obtained a Master's of Arts in Theology and a Master's of Divinity at Fuller Theological Seminary. His areas of ministry interest include New Testament studies and church visioning processes. When he's not busy pastoring you can find him rooting on his Alma mater's football and basketball teams or curled up with a good book.
