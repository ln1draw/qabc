Beliefs and Values

Beliefs

  • We believe that God is good, full of grace, mercy, long suffering and love
  • We believe that God's love for the world is expressed by the life, death and resurrection of Jesus Christ, God's only Son.
  • We believe that God's love communicated to us through the Holy Spirit empowers us to love God, our neighbors and ourselves.

Values

  • We show God's love to our community by embracing social justice and performing small yet meaningful acts of kindness
  • We embrace racial, cultural and generational diversity
  • We emphasize the thoughtful, rigorous study of scripture
  • We hold to the belief that any and all questions are welcomed by God
